Mahsa Saeidi

My research interest lies at the intersection of privacy, security, and human-computer interaction in smart homes focusing on three aspects: i) studying end-users’ perceptions and expectations of privacy in smart homes, ii) discovering information flows from smart-home apps, iii) developing a new approach to enable end users to manage their privacy easier. I obtained my PhD in Computer Science from Electrical Engineering & Computer Science department at Oregon State University.
